A Simplified Guide to Pregnancy and Infant Care

This is a simple guide for mothers and fathers who are expecting a child. This source will contain important information that can help both partners understand what is going on during throughout the pregnancy. Additionally, this source will help both partners make better decisions based on the given circumstances. The other portion of the source will help partners take care of their newborn and infant up to year. I hope this information will help you on your journey of parenthood.
